How it works

Always on.
Never in the way.

01
Install once

Your publisher sends a link or you download from inkstain.ai. One click. Inkstain installs silently and starts recording. You go back to writing. That's the entire setup.

02
Your Trail accretes

Every session is witnessed. Every AI interaction logged by event, not content. No keystrokes captured. No screen recording. No document reading. The Trail is yours and lives on your machine.

03
You hold the record

When you're ready — at submission, at publication, or when someone makes an accusation — you generate your certificate. You choose what to share, when, and with whom.

What Inkstain will never do

  • Read your writing
  • Stream data to your publisher
  • Capture your keystrokes
  • Produce a verdict about your work
  • Record your screen
  • Share your Trail without your approval
  • Sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
  • Work against you. Not once.
